Suggested changes:
-change name - not almanac
-recommended options not only based on amount of time but dropdown menu for different purposes (time, walking/getting steps in, avoiding bus/train routes, avoiding inclement weather, etc.)
-incorporate step counter
-incorporate change departure/arrival time
-need more clarity on notices/delays
-obvious marker on map of where you are (choose avatar or symbol)
-alert before stop could have different dropdown options (flash across screen, short buzz, sound, image, etc. - option for greater detail or notice especially beneficial for travellers)
-maybe different modes for daily commuter, casual rider, out of towner, more or less info about how to use transit system (example - out of towners get reminder or notice that you should exit your seat and walk towards the doors BEFORE you get to your stop bc the train opens/cloes quickly, etc.)
